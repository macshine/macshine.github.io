Mac Shine

I'm a cognitive neuroscientist interested in understanding the mechanisms of cognition and attention using functional brain imaging, both in health and disease. I am currently working with Russell Poldrack at Stanford University to understand the factors that drive the network-level reorganization of the human brain. I received my PhD from Sydney University in 2013 (PI: Simon JG Lewis), my MBBS from Sydney University in 2007 and my BSc from the University of Sydney in 2003.
